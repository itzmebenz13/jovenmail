Privacy Policy

1. Absolute Anonymity
We do not log, store, or analyze the contents of your emails. The system acts solely as a real-time routing layer.

2. No Database Persistence
Emails displayed in this dashboard are fetched directly through the secure realtime layer and are automatically cleared from the ephemeral cache. Once you close the session, the local view is permanently gone.

3. Native Gmail Infrastructure
By leveraging Gmail's native dot-blindness feature, we ensure all routing relies entirely on Google's enterprise-grade infrastructure. Your actual Gmail address remains completely hidden from the sender.

4. Third-Party Services
We use Supabase for realtime state synchronization. No personally identifiable information (PII) beyond the temporary dot-variant is transmitted.
